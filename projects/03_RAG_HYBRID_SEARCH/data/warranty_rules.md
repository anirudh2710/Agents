# Warranty & Replacement Terms

## 1. Coverage Duration & Terms
- All electronic products purchased from our official store carry a **1-Year Limited Manufacturer Warranty** against hardware defects and workmanship failures.
- Refurbished items carry a **90-Day Limited Warranty**.
- Warranty coverage is non-transferable and applies only to the original purchaser with valid order confirmation.

## 2. Exclusions
- Does not cover accidental damage, liquid spills, unauthorized disassembly, normal wear and tear, or cosmetic scratches.

## 3. Claiming a Replacement (RMA Process)
- To initiate a warranty claim, submit a request with proof of purchase and a short video/photo demonstrating the failure.
- If approved, an Return Merchandise Authorization (RMA) label is generated.
- Replacement units are shipped within 2 business days of defective unit receipt at our service facility.
