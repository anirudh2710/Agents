# Shipping & Order Support Guidelines

## 1. Domestic & International Shipping Rates
- **Standard Domestic (USA)**: Free on orders over $50; $4.99 flat rate for orders under $50. Estimated delivery: 3-5 business days.
- **Express Domestic**: $14.99 flat rate. Estimated delivery: 1-2 business days.
- **International Shipping**: Flat rate of $24.99 for Canada & Mexico; $39.99 for Europe & Asia. Customs, duties, and import taxes are the responsibility of the recipient.

## 2. Order Cancellations & Modifications
- Orders can be cancelled or modified within **1 hour of placement** directly through the Customer Portal.
- Once an order status changes to "Fulfillment / In Transit", it cannot be cancelled. The customer must initiate a standard return upon receipt.

## 3. Package Tracking & Delivery Issues
- Tracking information is emailed within 24 hours of dispatch.
- If a package is marked as delivered but missing, please allow 24 hours for carrier delay before filing a Lost Package Claim. Claims must be submitted within 14 days of estimated delivery.
