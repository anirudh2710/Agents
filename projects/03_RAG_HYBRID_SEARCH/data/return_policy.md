# Enterprise E-Commerce Return & Refund Policy

## 1. Return Windows by Category
- **Electronics & Open-Box Items**: 30-day return window from date of delivery.
- **Apparel & Fashion (Clothing, Shoes)**: 60-day return window from date of delivery.
- **Home & Kitchen Appliances**: 45-day return window from date of delivery.
- **Final Sale & Clearance Items**: Non-returnable and non-refundable.

## 2. Item Condition Requirements
- **Sealed / Unopened Items**: Eligible for a 100% full refund to original payment method with free return shipping.
- **Open-Box / Opened Items**: Must include all original packaging, cables, and accessories. Subject to inspection upon receipt.
- **Restocking Fee**: A flat 15% restocking fee applies to open-box electronics unless the item arrived defective or damaged.

## 3. Refund Processing Timelines
- Once the returned item is inspected at our warehouse (typically 2-3 business days after arrival), refunds are issued within 3-5 business days to your original payment method.
- Store credit options are available for immediate processing upon carrier scan.
